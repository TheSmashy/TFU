## Overview

The **TFU-F6** is an **ultralight field tool** built for one specific job:

> Deliver a high-CRI hotspot with practical reach — in a truly pocketable package.

This is not a flood light and not a sustained-output platform.  
The F6 exists for **mobility-first field use**, where weight, size, and speed matter.

Built on the **Convoy T6 platform** with a regulated **5A buck driver**, it provides stable output and a clean, usable beam in a compact 14500 form factor.

---

## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Nichia 519A  
- **CCT:** 5000K (high CRI)  
- **Driver:** 5A buck (regulated)  
- **Battery:** 14500 Li-ion  
- **Mode Group:** 1% → 20% → 100%  
- **Memory:** ON  
- **Effective Reach:** ~75 meters  

---

## Use Case

The F6 is designed for:

- Field movement with minimal load  
- Target identification at moderate distance  
- General-purpose field carry with real reach  

This is the light you carry when:

- You want **less weight than a full field light**  
- You still need **a defined hotspot**  
- You value **speed and simplicity over endurance**

---

## Mode Philosophy

### Default Mode (Group 5): 1% → 20% → 100%

Optimized for use with a **Vapcell K10**

- **1%** — admin / close work  
- **20%** — primary working mode (~50m effective)  
- **100%** — maximum output (~75m PID), short-duration  

This is the intended configuration.

---

## Beam Profile

The F6 is tuned for a **balanced field beam**:

- Large, bright hotspot  
- Controlled spill for awareness  
- High CRI for accurate identification  

The SMO reflector gives the 519A **more reach than typical EDC configurations**, without turning it into a pencil beam.

At output levels:

- **10%** — wide, usable close-range light  
- **20%** — primary field output with reach  
- **100%** — maximum intensity for short bursts  

---

## Performance Notes

- Regulated output — stable brightness  
- Rapid thermal rise at 100%  
- 35% is the intended sustained working level  

This is a **burst-capable tool**, not a continuous high-output light.
