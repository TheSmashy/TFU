## **Assembly Notes**

1. **Spacer fit:** Flat contact to pill; no wobble.
2. **MCPCB:** Thin MX-4 layer; even torque; optic sits flush.
3. **Driver:**  
   - Set Group 10  
   - Verify ground ring continuity  
   - CS109 dot beneath driver for stability
4. **Springs:** Bypass both (22 AWG).
5. **Optic stack:** 10508 lightly contacts lens; black o-ring adds compression and beam refinement.
6. **Thread security:** Loctite 242 on retaining rings and clip screws.

---

## **QA Checklist**

- Mode order correct; memory off
- Tail current verified on all modes
- **15-minute 100% thermal test** (expect ~60 °C head max)
- Uniform beam on white wall
- Outdoor beam check at 10–20 m
- LVP warning and cutoff verified
- No rattle; optic not pinched
- **Drop test:** 1 m onto concrete (controlled)

---

## **Maintenance**

- Re-lube threads quarterly
- Keep spare **10508** optic in kit
- Annual driver re-torque and MCPCB inspection
- Rotate and storage-charge cells per TFU policy
