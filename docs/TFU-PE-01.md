## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Nichia 519A (R9080)  
- **CCT:** 4500K (neutral-warm, slightly below BBL)  
- **Driver:** 3V 5A buck (regulated)  
- **Optic:** 15° matte TIR  
- **Host:** Convoy S2+ (MAO)  
- **Configuration:** 18350 titanium tube  
- **Switch:** Metal button (no LED)  
- **Clip:** Stonewashed titanium (tuned retention)  

---

## Beam Character

This is where the PE-01 earns its place.

The **519A 4500K + 15° matte TIR** combination produces a beam that is:

- Slightly warm-leaning without going amber  
- Clean, with minimal tint shift  
- Soft-edged with a defined center  
- Easy on the eyes in extended use  

The emitter binning and optic pairing pull the tint **just below the BBL**, giving:

- Better contrast on real surfaces  
- More natural rendering of materials  
- Reduced harshness compared to cooler or greener beams  

This is a **working beam**, not a show beam.

## Output & Modes

**Mode Group:** 1% / 10% / 35% / 100%  
**Memory:** Off  

- **1%** — Night-safe, map reading, zero disruption  
- **10%** — General indoor movement  
- **35%** — Primary working level (recommended default)  
- **100%** — Burst output when needed  

This light is designed to live at **10–35%**, where it is stable, efficient, and comfortable.
