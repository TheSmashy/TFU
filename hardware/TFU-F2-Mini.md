# TFU-F2 Mini – Compact Field Light

![TFU Logo](../TFU-LOGO.png)

**Series:** TFU-F (Field)  
**Model:** TFU-F2 Mini  
**Status:** Production Variant  
**Mission Profile:**  
Compact field and utility light designed to provide TFU-F2 performance characteristics in a smaller, more portable form factor.

---

## Overview

The **TFU-F2 Mini** is a scaled variant of the F2 platform, built on the **Convoy M1 (18650)** host.

It maintains the same emitter, driver philosophy, and interface as the full-size F2, while reducing size and weight for easier carry.

This is not a downgrade. It is a **role shift** toward:
- everyday carry
- secondary field use
- compact utility work

---

## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Nichia 519A  
- **CCT:** 5000K (neutral white)  
- **Driver:** 5A buck (regulated)  
- **Battery:** 18650 (Molicel M35A recommended)  
- **Lens:** AR-coated glass  
- **Switch:** Forward clicky (16mm)  
- **UI:** Mode Group 10 (1% / 10% / 35% / 100%)  
- **Memory:** ON  

---

## Hardware Configuration

### Platform
- **Host:** Convoy M1  
- **Colorway:** Black / Tan  
- **Form Factor:** 18650  

The M1 provides a compact, durable platform with a slightly reduced thermal envelope compared to the M21A.

---

### Emitter and Beam

- **LED:** Nichia 519A  
- **CCT:** 5000K  

The beam profile remains consistent with the F2 design philosophy:
- Defined hotspot  
- Controlled spill  

Slightly tighter feel due to host geometry.

---

### Driver and Output

- **Driver:** 5A Buck (regulated)

Delivers:
- Stable output  
- Efficient runtime  
- Predictable performance  

Thermal limits are lower than the full-size F2, but performance remains strong within the platform constraints.

---

### Switch and Interface

- **Switch:** Forward clicky  
- **Mode Group:** 10  
- **Levels:** 1% / 10% / 35% / 100%  
- **Memory:** Enabled  

Matches the F2 exactly for consistency across the platform.

---

## Battery Guidance

- **Recommended Cell:** Molicel M35A  
- **Type:** 18650  

The M35A prioritizes:
- Runtime  
- stability  
- longevity  

while remaining fully capable at 5A.

---

## Positioning

The TFU-F2 Mini is a **compact field and EDC variant** of the F2.

It is best suited for:
- daily carry
- secondary light roles
- situations where size and weight matter more than maximum runtime

---

## Relationship to TFU-F2

- Same emitter  
- Same driver philosophy  
- Same interface  

Reduced size, reduced runtime, increased portability.

The F2 Mini complements the F2 rather than replacing it.
