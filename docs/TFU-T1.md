## Overview

The **TFU-T1** is a hardened tactical light designed for **longer-range positive identification (PID)** and dependable field use.

Built on the **Convoy M2 platform**, the T1 pairs a **Luminus SFT40 emitter** with a **regulated 8A buck driver** to produce a powerful, high-intensity beam optimized for identification and situational awareness.

The configuration is intentionally simple:

- **Immediate access to maximum brightness**
- **Minimal, predictable mode set**
- **No mode memory**

Additional hardening modifications improve **thermal transfer, electrical efficiency, and mechanical stability**, making the T1 a dependable tool rather than a novelty light.

_Primary mission:_ deliver **reliable, high-intensity illumination for identification and tactical awareness**.

---

## At a Glance

- **Host:** Convoy M2  
- **Emitter:** Luminus **SFT40**  
- **CCT:** 6500K  
- **Driver:** **8A buck driver**  
- **Lens:** AR-coated glass  
- **Switch:** Forward clicky  
- **Clip:** Black **deep-carry clip**  
- **Battery:** 18650 Li-ion  
- **Mode Group:** **100% / 20% / 1%**  
- **Mode Memory:** Off  

The light **always activates at 100%**.

---

## Hardening Modifications

The TFU-T1 includes several build modifications intended to improve durability and performance:

- **MX-4 thermal compound** under the MCPCB for improved heat transfer  
- **Loctite applied to retaining rings** to prevent loosening under recoil or vibration  
- **Tail spring bypass** to reduce electrical resistance and improve current delivery  
- **AR glass lens** for improved light transmission  
- **Black deep-carry clip** replacing the standard silver clip for better carry and reduced visibility  

These changes are small individually but collectively improve the **reliability and efficiency of the light under real use**.

## Thermal & Output Notes

The **TFU-T1 prioritizes identification performance and beam intensity**.

### 100%

- Maximum output  
- Intended for **PID and burst illumination**  
- Heat builds quickly  

### 20%

- Excellent working brightness  
- Much lower heat generation  
- Suitable for extended use  

### 1%

- Administrative lighting  
- Map reading  
- Close-range tasks  

**TFU recommendation:**

Use **100% for identification bursts** and **20% for sustained use**.

If the light becomes uncomfortable to hold, step down.

## Using the TFU-T1

- **Half-press (momentary):** cycle modes  
- **Full click:** on/off  
- **Forward clicky:** allows **momentary activation for signaling or tactical use**

Mode order:

100% → 20% → 1%

Mode memory is **disabled**, ensuring the light **always starts at maximum output**.
