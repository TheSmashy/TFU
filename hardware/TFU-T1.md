# TFU-T1

**Series:** T (Tactical / High Output)

**Role:** Pocket tactical / high-intensity field light  
**Status:** Active build

---

## Overview

The **TFU-T1** is a compact high-output light built on the **Convoy M2 platform**, configured for strong throw, immediate activation, and reliable field performance.

Using the **Luminus SFT40 6500K emitter** driven by an **8A regulated buck driver**, the T1 delivers a tight, high-intensity beam with excellent reach for its size. The deeper M2 reflector produces a defined hotspot and usable spill, making the light suitable for outdoor navigation, property checks, and general tactical use.

A **forward clicky switch** enables momentary activation, while the driver is configured for **Mode Group 6 (100 → 20 → 1%) with memory disabled**, ensuring the light always activates at full output.

The result is a simple, predictable interface optimized for rapid access to maximum output.

## TFU Build Modifications

Each TFU-T1 is assembled with several reliability and performance upgrades:

### Thermal Optimization
- **MX-4 thermal compound** applied beneath the MCPCB for improved heat transfer

### Electrical Efficiency
- **Tail spring bypass** using silicone wire to reduce resistance and improve high-current performance

### Mechanical Hardening
- **Loctite applied to brass retaining rings** to prevent loosening under recoil or repeated use

### Interface Upgrade
- **Forward clicky switch installed** for momentary activation and tactical use

### Carry Optimization
- **Factory clip removed**
- **Deep carry clip installed** for improved pocket carry and reduced profile

## Interface

The TFU-T1 uses **Mode Group 6** with memory disabled.

| Click Sequence | Output |
|----------------|--------|
| 1 Click | **100%** |
| Half-press | 20% |
| Half-press | 1% |

The light **always starts at 100%**, ensuring predictable operation under stress.
