## Beam & Use Profile

The E4 is tuned for **clean task lighting**, not exaggerated throw or excessive flood.

- Smooth, well-defined hotspot  
- Even, usable spill with no harsh cutoff  
- No visible rings, tint shift, or artifacts  

The **519A 5000K** emitter provides excellent clarity and color accuracy, making the E4 particularly effective for:

- Equipment and vehicle inspection  
- Technical work and PID tasks  
- Indoor and outdoor navigation  
- General utility and EDC use
