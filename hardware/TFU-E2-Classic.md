## **UI & Power**
- **Mode Group:** 10 → 1% / 10% / 35% / 100%  
- **Memory:** **OFF**  
- **LVP:** Warn ~3.0 V / cutoff ~2.8 V  
- **Cell Policy:**  
  - Use **30Q** for best turbo  
  - Use **M35A** for the longest 35% mode runs  
  - Store all cells at **3.7–3.8 V**

---

## **Assembly Notes**
1. **Spacer:** Verify clean contact to pill; no wobble.  
2. **MCPCB:** MX-4 thin film; torque evenly; confirm optic sits flush.  
3. **Driver:**  
   - Flash or set Group 10  
   - Confirm ground ring continuity  
   - Dot of CS109 under board for thermal transfer  
4. **Springs:** Bypass both (22 AWG).  
5. **Optic Fit:** 10508 should lightly kiss the lens; black o-ring adds compression + beam quality.  
6. **Thread Lock:** 242 on retaining rings + clip screws.

---

## **QA Checklist**
- Mode order correct; memory off  
- Tail currents verified on all modes  
- **15-minute 100% thermal run** (expect ~60°C head max)  
- Beam uniform on white wall  
- Outdoor beam check at 10–20 m  
- LVP warn/cutoff validated  
- No rattle; optic not pinched  
- Drop test: 1 m onto concrete (controlled)

---

## **Maintenance**
- Re-lube threads quarterly  
- Keep a spare **10508** optic in kit  
- Annual driver re-torque + MCPCB inspection  
- Rotate and storage-charge cells per policy
