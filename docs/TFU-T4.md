## Overview

The **TFU-T4** is a compact, high-intensity field light built for distance and clarity.

Using a **Luminus SFT-25R emitter** driven by a **regulated 5A buck driver**, the T4 produces a tight, high-candela beam while maintaining practical spill for real-world use.

This is the step up from the TFU-F4 — more reach, more intensity, without becoming specialized or difficult to use.

---

## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Luminus SFT-25R  
- **CCT:** 5000K  
- **Driver:** 5A buck (regulated)  
- **Battery:** 14500 Li-ion  
- **Modes (default):** 1% → 20% → 100%  

---

## Beam Profile

The T4 is tuned for **balanced throw and usability**.

- Defined hotspot with strong intensity  
- Usable spill for situational awareness  
- Longer reach than high-CRI work lights  

This makes it ideal for:

- Outdoor use  
- Property checks  
- Long-range illumination  
- General “need to see farther” tasks  

---

## Performance Notes

- The 5A buck driver provides **stable output and efficiency**
- Noticeably higher intensity than high-CRI emitters
- Maintains useful spill while extending range

100% mode:

- High output and high intensity  
- Increased thermal load  
- Best used in short bursts when maximum visibility is needed  

---

## Build Features

- AR-coated glass
- MX-4 thermal interface under MCPCB  
- Loctite-secured retaining rings  
- Tail spring bypass (reduced resistance, improved performance)  
- Black deep-carry clip  

---

## Use Philosophy

The T4 is designed to be:

- Capable  
- Direct  
- Immediately useful  

It delivers more reach than a standard field light without requiring special handling or knowledge.

---

## Battery Guidance

Recommended:

- Vapcell F12 / F15 for balanced use  
- Higher-drain cells can be used but are not required  

---

## Care and Handling

- Avoid prolonged use on 100% in confined spaces  
- Allow cooling between extended high-output use  
- Be aware of beam intensity at close range
