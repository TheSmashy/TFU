## Overview

## Output & Thermal Characteristics

The E0 prioritizes regulation and beam quality over peak lumen numbers.

### Mode Roles

- **1%** ‚Äî Night-safe, close inspection, map reading
- **20%** ‚Äî Bench work, diagnostics, general tasks
- **100%** ‚Äî Full clarity for short-duration, high-detail tasks

At 1.5A, the E0 remains thermally controlled and efficient.  
Heat rise is moderate and appropriate for the host size.

If the light becomes uncomfortable to hold, power down and allow cooling.

## Care & Handling

- Install and remove the battery from the **tailcap end**
- Keep threads clean and lightly lubricated
- Do not over-tighten
- Store with the cell at partial charge for long-term storage
