# TFU-F5 – Field Spotting Light

**Series:** TFU-F (Field)  
**Model:** TFU-F5  
**Status:** Production Ready  

---

## Mission Profile

Compact field spotting light designed for **distance identification and reach**. Built around a high-intensity emitter and smooth reflector, the F5 delivers a tight, neutral beam for positive ID at range with usable runtime at practical levels.

---

## Build Overview

The TFU-F5 is a **throw-first platform** based on the Convoy M21A host.

This configuration prioritizes:

- High candela and tight beam geometry  
- Neutral tint for real-world identification  
- Stable output at reduced levels for sustained use  

The result is a light that excels at **spotting, scanning, and confirming**, rather than general-purpose illumination.

## Performance Profile

### Working Level (10%)

- Effective spotting to ~100 meters  
- Approx. 5 hours runtime (P42A)  
- Stable output with minimal thermal impact  
- Intended primary field mode  

### Maximum Output (100%)

- ~600 meter reach  
- High-intensity hotspot for rapid identification  
- Use in short bursts for scanning and confirmation  

---

## Beam Characteristics

The **SFT-25R + SMO** configuration produces a **tight, high-candela hotspot** with controlled spill:

- Neutral 5000K tint for accurate color rendering at distance  
- Strong contrast compared to typical cool white throwers  
- Minimal beam artifacts  
- Enough spill to maintain awareness around the hotspot  

This is a **precision distance tool**, not a flood light.

---

## Power and Thermal Notes

- The 8A buck driver delivers strong output with good efficiency  
- The M21A host provides sufficient thermal mass for controlled use at high output  
- Best performance is achieved using high-drain cells (P42A recommended)  

At 100%, the light will generate significant heat and should be used in **short, intentional bursts**.

### Performace and Runtime

**TFU-F5 SFT-25R 5000K 8A Buck and P42A (4200mAh)**
| Mode  | Output (Lumens) | Runtime (Est.) | Behavior |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|----------|
| Low (1%)   | ~15–20 lm     | ~7–10 days     | True low, slightly brighter baseline due to emitter |
| Med (10%)  | ~150–180 lm   | ~20–30 hrs     | Clean, fully regulated, good general use |
| High (35%) | ~500–650 lm   | ~6–10 hrs      | Strong working mode, noticeable heat but sustainable |
| Turbo (100%) | ~1400–1800 lm | ~1–1.5 hrs total* | ~30–90 sec peak → aggressive stepdown → stabilizes ~40–50% |  

>“Designed for high-intensity output. Turbo provides maximum reach for short durations, stepping down quickly to a sustained working level. Lower modes remain fully regulated.”

## Use Case

The TFU-F5 is designed for:

- Field spotting and identification  
- Perimeter checks  
- Outdoor scanning at distance  
- Situations where **reach and clarity matter more than flood**  

Not intended as a general indoor or urban-use light.
