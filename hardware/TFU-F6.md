# TFU-F6 – Ultralight Field Light

**Series:** TFU-F (Field)  
**Model:** TFU-F6  
**Status:** Production Ready  
**Mission Profile:**  
Compact, lightweight field light delivering a high-CRI hotspot with usable throw. Designed for mobility, quick deployment, and practical PID without the weight of a full-size platform.

---

## Overview

The **TFU-F6** is an ultralight field light built on the **Convoy T6** platform, configured for a **focused, high-CRI beam** using a single 519A emitter and SMO reflector.

This light exists to answer a specific problem:

> You want real field capability without carrying a full-size light.

The F6 provides a **large, bright hotspot with clean spill**, giving effective illumination out to ~75 meters while remaining pocketable and easy to carry.

It is not a sustained-output platform. It is a **mobility-first field tool**.

---

## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Nichia 519A  
- **CCT:** 5000K (neutral white, high CRI)  
- **Driver:** 5A Buck  
- **Reflector:** SMO (smooth)  
- **Switch:** Reverse clicky with spring bypass  
- **Clip:** Black deep carry  
- **Recommended Cell:** Vapcell K10 (14500)  
- **Mode Group:** 1 (1% / 10% / 35% / 100%)  
- **Memory:** ON  

---

## Beam Profile

- **Hotspot:** Large, bright, well-defined  
- **Spill:** Usable, balanced, not overly floody  
- **Character:** High-CRI spot with practical field reach  

The SMO reflector tightens the beam enough to make the 519A behave like a **true working spot**, not just a floody EDC emitter.

- **10%:** General navigation, close work  
- **35%:** Primary working mode, effective to ~50m  
- **100%:** Burst output for PID to ~75m  

---

## Performance Notes

- **100%:** Strong output, rapid thermal rise  
- **35%:** Sustained working high, stable and usable  
- **10% and below:** Long runtime, EDC-friendly  

This is a **burst-capable light**, not a thermal tank.

---

## Form Factor

- True **pocketable field light**  
- Lightweight and fast to deploy  
- Deep carry clip keeps it low profile  

Compared to larger F-series lights:

- Less thermal mass  
- Less sustained output  
- Significantly better carry comfort  

---

## Positioning

The **TFU-F6** fills the gap between:

- EDC lights that lack reach  
- Full-size field lights that are overkill to carry  

**Use it when:**

- You want a **real hotspot in a small package**  
- You are moving light and fast  
- You still need **PID beyond typical EDC range**  

**Do not expect:**

- Long sustained 100% output  
- Large thermal reserves  

---

## Configuration Notes

- **Memory ON** is intentional for this platform  
  - Enables predictable return to working mode  
  - Supports real-world field use with a reverse clicky  

- **Spring bypass** improves current delivery and responsiveness  

---

## Summary

The **TFU-F6** is a purpose-built ultralight field light:

- High CRI  
- Real hotspot  
- Pocketable  
- Fast, simple, effective  

It trades thermal mass for mobility, and does it on purpose.
