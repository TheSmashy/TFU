## Overview

The **TFU-T3** is a compact tactical light built for **positive identification (PID)** and rapid illumination.

Using a **Luminus SFT40 emitter** driven by a **potted 8A buck driver**, the T3 produces a tight, high-intensity beam from a compact S6 platform.

The configuration is intentionally simple and predictable:

- **Immediate access to maximum brightness**
- **Minimal mode set**
- **No mode memory**

_Primary mission:_ deliver **instant high-output illumination for identification and situational awareness**.

---

## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Luminus SFT40  
- **CCT:** 6500K
- **Driver:** TFU 8A potted buck driver
- **Lens:** AR-coated glass
- **Lens O-ring:** Black
- **Switch:** Forward clicky
- **Clip:** TFU deep-carry clip
- **Battery:** 18650 Li-ion
- **Mode Group:** **100% / 20% / 1%**
- **Mode Memory:** Off

The light **always activates at 100%**.

---

## Battery Notes

### Recommended

**Molicel P30B**

- High-drain cell
- Excellent voltage stability
- Ideal for the 8A driver

Provides the most consistent output and regulation.

### Compatible Alternative

**Samsung 25R**

- Older cell chemistry
- Still capable of supporting the driver
- Slightly reduced performance compared to modern cells

Works reliably but the P30B is preferred.

## Battery Install Notice

Slide the TFU clip off the body to remove the tailcap and install the battery.  

The head may also be unscrewed for front battery installation.  

---

## Thermal & Output Notes

The **TFU-T3 prioritizes immediate brightness over extended runtime**.

### 100%
- Maximum output
- Intended for **PID and burst use**
- Heat rises quickly

### 20%
- Practical working level
- Excellent brightness with much lower heat
- Suitable for sustained use

### 1%
- Administrative light
- Map reading
- Low-light tasks

**TFU recommendation:**

Treat **100% as a burst mode**.

Use **20% for sustained operation** when possible.

If the light becomes uncomfortable to hold, step down.

---

## Using the TFU-T3

- **Half-press (momentary):** cycle modes  
- **Full click:** on/off  
- **Forward clicky:** supports momentary activation for signaling or tactical use

Mode order:
100% → 20% → 1%

Mode memory is **disabled**, ensuring the light **always starts at maximum output**.
