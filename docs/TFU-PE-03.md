## At a Glance

- **Emitter:** Triple Nichia 519A (R9080)  
- **CCT:** 4000K (neutral-warm, high usability)  
- **Driver:** 3V 5A buck (regulated)  
- **Optic:** Carclo 10511 (frosted medium)  
- **Host:** Convoy S2+ (silver)  
- **Configuration:** 18350 copper tube  
- **Spacer:** Copper thermal spacer  
- **Switch:** Silver steel button (no LED)  
- **Clip:** Stonewashed titanium (tuned retention)

## Beam Character

The PE-03 delivers one of the most **useful triple beams** in the lineup.

The **519A 4000K + Carclo 10511** pairing produces:

- A neutral-warm beam that works indoors and outdoors  
- High CRI with accurate color rendering  
- A soft, blended hotspot with wide usable spill  
- No harsh transitions or distracting artifacts  

Compared to tighter optics, the 10511 gives:

- More forgiving beam shape  
- Better close-range usability  
- Comfortable extended viewing  

This is a **true work beam** — wide, clean, and natural.
