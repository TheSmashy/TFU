## Overview

The **TFU-T2B** is a premium everyday carry flashlight built on the Brass T3 platform.

Designed around a warm, high-CRI Nichia 519A emitter, the T2B emphasizes natural color rendition, simple operation, and dependable performance over maximum output. Its solid brass construction develops a unique patina with use, making every light one of a kind.

The T2B is equally at home opening electrical panels, walking the dog, finding dropped hardware, or sitting in your pocket for years.

## Driver & UI

- **Driver:** 1.5A regulated
- **Mode Group:** 5
- **Modes:** 1% → 20% → 100%
- **Memory:** On (resumes last mode)
- **Switch:** Reverse clicky

### Quick Start

From OFF:

- Full press for ON
- Light tap to cycle modes
- Full press for OFF

For transport or long-term storage, loosen the tailcap approximately ¼ turn to mechanically lock out the light.

### Battery Compatibility  

The TFU-T2B ships with a rechargeable Vapcell F15 14500 lithium-ion battery for maximum performance.  

The regulated buck-boost driver also supports standard AA batteries (alkaline, NiMH, or lithium primary) for emergency or field use, with reduced output compared to the included 14500 cell.  

**For best performance and runtime, TFU recommends the included Vapcell F15 14500 rechargeable battery.**

## Why No Switch Bypass?

The TFU-T2B uses a regulated 1.5A driver.

Testing showed that a switch bypass offered no meaningful performance improvement while increasing complexity. TFU only incorporates modifications that provide measurable benefits, so the factory switch configuration is retained.

## Caring for Brass

Brass naturally oxidizes and develops a patina over time. This is normal and expected.

Some owners prefer the bright factory finish, while others enjoy the character that develops through everyday use. Either finish is correct.

If desired, the brass can be restored using a commercial brass polish and a soft cloth.
